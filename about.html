About Zarwood Gallery

The Art of Marquetry

Zarwood Gallery is dedicated to handcrafted marquetry artworks made from natural woods. Each piece is built slowly, layer by layer, using color, grain, texture, and contrast instead of paint.

The work begins with selecting woods for their natural tones and character, then cutting and assembling small pieces into a finished composition. The result is an artwork that carries both the image and the material story of the wood itself.
